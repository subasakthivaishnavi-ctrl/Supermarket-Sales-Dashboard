# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard

Interactive business intelligence dashboard developed using Looker Studio to analyze sales performance, customer growth, regional trends, product insights, and shipping analysis through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

## 🔗 Live Dashboard
👉 [View Interactive Dashboard](https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/323662f7-60a8-455b-8e25-b96c78ef37d2)

## 📌 Dashboard Highlights
- Analyzed **2.3M Total Sales** and **9.8K Total Orders**
- Monitored **Customer Growth %**, **Sales per Customer**, and **Average Order Value**
- Visualized revenue trends across months, regions, categories, and customer segments
- Built interactive filters for:
  - Region
  - Category
  - Segment
  - Ship Mode
  - Date Range
- Identified top-performing cities, products, and shipping methods

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Technology** category generated the highest revenue contribution
- **Consumer Segment** accounted for over 50% of total revenue
- **Standard Class** shipping mode recorded the highest sales contribution
- New York City emerged as the top revenue-generating city
- Revenue trends showed strong performance spikes during peak sales periods

## 📂 Business Use Case
This dashboard helps businesses monitor sales KPIs, analyze customer behavior, identify high-performing products and regions, optimize shipping performance, and support strategic decision-making through interactive analytics.
